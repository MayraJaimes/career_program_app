Philosophy

According to scientific studies, 40% of your happiness is directly under your control (as opposed to the other 60%, which is affected by genes and life circumstances). If you sacrifice happiness in your education or career, then you sacrifice the 40% of agency you have over your happiness during your waking hours. Here at YOLOFLO Coaching, we teach you how to develop skills that data shows the happiest people share: 1) the skill of setting meaningful life goals, and 2) the skill of being in flow. We distinguish between ends goals and means goals, between external rewards and internal rewards. We help you identify your core values to create your ideal lifestyle.

“Flow” is one of the most transformative concepts of positive psychology. Originally coined by psychologist Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi, “to be in flow” means to be fully engaged in an activity, as if time has no meaning. You know you’re in flow if you’re having fun, if you love the purpose behind what your doing, and if you bring originality to the table. Fun + Love + Originality = FLO. Integrating core values into your lifestyle and prioritizing states of flow not only increase your overall happiness, but these skills optimize your performance. The result is a more fulfilling academic/professional career - with a higher paycheck down the road. Don’t wait to have a midlife crisis. Choose to be happy now. Get your creative juices flowing.
